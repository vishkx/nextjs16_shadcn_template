## Code of Conduct

This project follows a simple, inclusionâ€‘focused Code of Conduct to help keep the community welcoming and respectful.

By participating in this project, you agree to:

- **Be respectful**: Treat everyone with kindness and consideration.
- **Assume good intent**: Start from the assumption that others are trying their best.
- **Use inclusive language**: Avoid hateful, harassing, or discriminatory language or behavior.
- **Respect boundaries**: If someone asks you to stop a certain behavior, stop.
- **Keep discussions constructive**: Focus on problems, not people.

### Unacceptable behavior

Examples of behavior that will not be tolerated include:

- Harassment, bullying, or personal attacks.
- Hate speech or discriminatory remarks.
- Sexualized language or imagery, or unwelcome sexual attention.
- Deliberate intimidation, stalking, or threats of violence.

### Reporting issues

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior:

- You can contact the project maintainer privately (for example, via email or direct message).
- Provide as much context as you can (links, screenshots, description of what happened).

The maintainer will review the report, keep it as confidential as possible, and take appropriate action, which may include warnings or removal of participation privileges.

### Scope

This Code of Conduct applies:

- Within the project repository (issues, discussions, pull requests, etc.).
- In any community spaces related to this project (chat, social media groups, etc.).
